    • Eyre Square

    Eyre Square is located at the centre of the town and is the first thing you see when leaving the train station. there is truly no better introduction to this city. Eyre Square consists mostly of a small park. there is spacious foothpaths throughout the park to easily maneuver around it. the park is surrounded by many flags representing the different tribes of Galway, there are ramps placed around the area to get near the bus stops which is very helpful and saves having to go the long way around.
