    • Salthill

    Salthill is on the outskirts of the city but is an absolutley essential place to visit when going to Galway. While the beach itself is not very accessible, the veiw from the sidewalks alone make up for it. There are also touching tributes to the Famine that are amazing to visit in person. The hotels in the area as well as a select few B&Bs are very accessable, however some of the smaller B&Bs may not be as accessible as they should be.
