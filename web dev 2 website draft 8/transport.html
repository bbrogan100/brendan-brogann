    Getting to and around Galway has made amazing amazing progress in terms of accessibility in the last few years. One way of getting to Galway woul be through the train with Iarnrod Eireann. the trains offer ramps to enter and get off the carraiges, as well as wheelchair accessable bathrooms.

    while in Galway, one of the quickest ways to get around would be the TFI (Transport For Ireland) Bus routes. the many different routes can bring you virtually anywhere in Galway City. The busses have ramps integrated into their entrances which makes entering and getting off the bus easier than ever.
